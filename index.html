Who’s That?

An aspiring digital product designer based in Berlin. Read a bit more about how I got where I am or jump straight to my selected works.

I have a bachelor’s degree in physics from the Freie Universität Berlin. I studied physics mostly to prove to myself that I could do it. Reasearch for my thesis involved beam time at the BESSY II synchrotron radiation source.

This is also where I picked up a lot of useful skills like Python, Java and JavaScript, and how to process and visualise data properly.

Right now, I’m enrolled in a bachelor’s program in communication design at HTW Berlin.

Recently I’ve been working on my JavaScript skills in combination with some fancy UI / UX stuff. I love this kind of work because it exposes me to awesome tools and technology like Figma, Framer, THREE.js and D3.js.

On the side, I am a chairman at Sehen und Ernten.

I also proudly bartend part-time at Alter Roter Löwe Rein. I believe it’s important to know how to make a decent gin fizz and how to correctly pour a beer.
I really enjoy the comradery of working in a great team, but I will say that the hours are pretty terrible.

Some other usefull skills include repairing bicycles, fluent academic English (TOEFL score of 112⁄120) and even a driver’s license!
